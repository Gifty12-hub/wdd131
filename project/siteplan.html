    • STEM Hub for Girls in Ghana.

    This is a dedicated platform that empowers young girls in Ghana to explore and excel in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM).

    Through interactive learning resources, mentorship, and hands-on projects, the hub fosters curiosity, innovation, and confidence in the next generation of female STEM leaders.

    The site serves as a central platform for inspiring and supporting young girls in Ghana to pursue careers in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM).

    It provides educational resources, hands-on project guides, mentorship opportunities, and success stories of women in STEM. Additionally, the site will feature workshops, events, and scholarship information to encourage participation and growth in STEM fields.

  • Are there any upcoming STEM workshops or mentorship programs I can join?

  • Can I connect with female role models in STEM for guidance and inspiration?
