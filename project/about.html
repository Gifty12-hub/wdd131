Our Vision

We envision a future where every girl in Ghana has the confidence, skills, and support needed to pursue STEM careers and contribute meaningfully to technological advancements and innovation.

Our Mission

Girls in STEM Ghana aims to inspire interest in STEM through engaging workshops and hands-on training sessions. The initiative provides mentorship and networking opportunities with female professionals in STEM fields, helping to guide and motivate young girls. It also equips participants with practical skills in coding, robotics, and problem-solving, empowering them to pursue careers in technology and engineering. Additionally, the program fosters a supportive community that encourages collaboration, continuous learning, and personal growth.

About Girls in STEM Ghana

Empowering the Next Generation of Female Innovators Girls in STEM Ghana is a dedicated initiative aimed at inspiring, educating, and empowering young Ghanaian girls to explore and excel in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). Our mission is to bridge the gender gap in STEM fields by providing access to quality education, mentorship, and hands-on learning experiences.

Why Girls in STEM Ghana?

Despite growing advancements in technology and science, women remain underrepresented in STEM fields. Girls in STEM Ghana is committed to breaking these barriers by encouraging early exposure to STEM education, promoting gender inclusivity, and ensuring equal opportunities for all.
